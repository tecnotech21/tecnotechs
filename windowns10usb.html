How To Make Bootable Windows 10 Flash Disk

Author:Abdulghani Koujan Writed On:9/5/2023

Step 1: Go To This Link That Sends You To Microsofts Offical Website

Step 2: Press to Download Tool Now

Step 3: Open It

Step 4: Accept The Terms

Step 5: Chose Create Installation Media

Step 6: Press Next

Step 7: Chose ISO File And Press Next

Step 8: Wait It To Download

Step 9: Download Rufus From This Button

Step 10: Open Rufus

Step 11: Chose Your Usb

Step 12: Chose Your Iso File

Step 13: If You Have Old Computer(BIOS) Choose MBR Or If You Have New Computer(UEFI) Choose GPT

Step 14: Press Start

When The Process Complete You Have Maked A Windows 10 Boot USB
